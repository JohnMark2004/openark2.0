OpenArk is an online library that provides students with access to a wide range of educational resources, including textbooks, research papers, and study guides. Our mission is to make learning more accessible and convenient for students and librarians.

OpenArk Features

Collections

Access textbooks, research papers, and study materials across all disciplines. Our library grows daily with new additions.

Advanced Search

Find exactly what you need with our advanced search features. Filter by category, author, year, and more to save time.

Bookmarks & Progress

Save your favorite books and track your reading progress. Pick up right where you left off, anytime, anywhere.

Personalized

Get book recommendations based on your year level and interests.

Text-to-Speech

Listen to your textbooks with our built-in text-to-speech feature. Perfect for auditory learners and multitasking.

Community

Engage with fellow students through book comments and discussions. Share insights and learn together.
